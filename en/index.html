R-Force Technology

Revolutionary therapy method for optimal results

How Does R-Force Work?

R-Force is a cutting-edge technology that uses precise impulses to stimulate muscle tissue. This leads to improved blood circulation, faster recovery and optimal muscle activation.

Who Is It Suitable For?

R-Force is ideal for patients with muscle tension, sports injuries, chronic pain and prevention of movement restrictions. Also for professional athletes for performance optimization.

Clinical Benefits

Faster healing, reduced pain, improved muscle strength, preventive effect, non-invasive and side-effect free. Scientifically proven and clinically tested.
